Lecture 18: Multiagent Decision Making¶

AIMA Chapter 18 — 1 hour¶

Listen — slide 2 Captions (VTT)

Learning Objectives¶

  • Define normal-form and extensive-form games

  • Find Nash equilibria

  • Understand cooperative game theory

  • Apply auctions, voting, bargaining

Listen — slide 3 Captions (VTT)

Normal-Form Games¶

  • Players: N agents

  • Actions: A_i for each

  • Payoffs: u_i(a₁,...,aₙ)

  • Matrix: 2-player, 2-action

Listen — slide 4 Captions (VTT)

Nash Equilibrium¶

  • No player wants to deviate unilaterally

  • Mixed strategy: Probability over actions

  • Existence: Every finite game has mixed Nash

Listen — slide 5 Captions (VTT)

Repeated Games¶

  • Stage game: Played repeatedly

  • Strategies: Tit-for-tat, grim trigger

  • Folk theorem: Many equilibria

Listen — slide 6 Captions (VTT)

Extensive Form¶

  • Game tree: Sequential moves

  • Information sets: What players know

  • Subgame perfect: Nash in every subgame

Listen — slide 7 Captions (VTT)

Cooperative Games¶

  • Coalitions: Subsets of players

  • Characteristic function: v(S) = value of coalition S

  • Core, Shapley value: Solution concepts

Listen — slide 8 Captions (VTT)

Auctions¶

  • English auction: Ascending

  • Vickrey: Second-price, truthful

  • Combinatorial: Multiple items

Listen — slide 9 Captions (VTT)

Voting¶

  • Social choice: Aggregate preferences

  • Arrow’s theorem: No perfect system

  • Strategic voting: Manipulation

Listen — slide 10 Captions (VTT)

Summary¶

  • Nash: No unilateral deviation

  • Repeated: Cooperation possible

  • Cooperative: Coalitions

  • Mechanisms: Auctions, voting
